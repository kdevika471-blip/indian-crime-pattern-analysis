About the Project 📖

Explaining the mission and datasets behind Indian Crime Pattern Analysis

Project Overview

The objective of this portal is to equip citizens and law enforcement agencies with a transparent, analytics-driven dashboard. By tracking reported crimes across states, we aim to visualize metrics and reveal emerging threats rapidly.

Dataset Description

The underlying data encompasses incident types (theft, assault, cybercrime, etc.), timestamps, involved demographics, and geolocation. The numbers shown here are derived from a combination of public safety records and synthesized test data to demonstrate the system's capabilities.

Importance of Crime Analytics

Predictive modeling and immediate data visualization reduce response times. Through continuous monitoring, authorities can preemptively allocate resources to high-risk zones, ultimately creating a safer society.
